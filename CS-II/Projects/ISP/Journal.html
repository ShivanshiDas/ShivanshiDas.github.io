QA (Quality Assurance) Engineer
Quality Assurance Engineers primarily focus on upholding industry standards and regulations for software and develop and analyse test scripts to maintain the quality of software. Quality Assurance Engineers are also responsible for ensuring that the final product is user friendly and assess test results to predict user behavior and possible bugs. Once again, my largest obstacle would be the technical portion of this role as I do not have much experience writing debugging scripts. However, I do have experience manually debugging my own programs and given my attention to detail and patient work style, I believe I would do quite well in this role.

Project Manager
Project Managers primarily focus on the scheduling, budgeting, execution, and delivery of software projects and see projects from start to finish. As an individual with a strong time management and communication skills, I can see myself doing well in this role. Product Manager
Product Managers primarily focus on consumer needs, market analysis, and competitors to develop a marketable product and improve the product performance. Product managers must have strong communication skills are they are the main bridge between the client and software development team and work towards launching a useful and marketable product. Release Engineer
