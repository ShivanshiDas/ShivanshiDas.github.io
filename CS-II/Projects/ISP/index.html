ShelterSafe
CONCEPT
ShelterSafe is an app designed for the people by the people with the intent of improving the organization and readability of information regarding shelters during natural disasters. With ShelterSafe, users can recieve not only real-time weather and natural disaster updates but also food, water, and general supply updates for nearby shelters. With such updates, ShelterSafe intends to guide users the shelter than can best accomodate for the user while also taking into consideration factors such as distance, road blocks, and the general path of the natural disaster to determine which shelter can be reached in a safe and timely manner. ShelterSafe depends on community inputs to send out local alerts regarding road blocks, damanged infrastructure, and the status of shelters to beter collect and share data with users. ShelterSafe is unique in that it will include a offline mode in which predictive algorithms backed by intensive research can predict the status of shelters for a certain period of time even after the user loses connection to a cell tower.
